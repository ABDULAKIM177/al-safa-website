Scholarship Assistance

We help students secure scholarships to make studying in China more affordable. Our team has extensive experience with various scholarship programs including Chinese Government Scholarships, University Scholarships, and Provincial Scholarships.

Chinese Government Scholarship

Full or partial scholarships offered by the Chinese government to international students. Covers tuition, accommodation, and provides living allowance.

  • Covers tuition fees
  • Free university dormitory or accommodation subsidy
  • Comprehensive medical insurance
  • Monthly stipend

University Scholarships

Scholarships directly offered by Chinese universities to attract outstanding international students.

  • Full or partial tuition waiver
  • Accommodation support
  • Living allowance in some cases
  • Available for both undergraduate and postgraduate studies

Provincial Scholarships

Scholarships offered by provincial governments in China to promote international education in their regions.

  • Varies by province
  • Often covers tuition fees
  • Sometimes includes accommodation
  • Great opportunity for specific regions of China
